The Science of Image Description

Accessibility Solutions, AI Blindspot Alignment, Knowledge Engineering

Prototype Description

Our research comes from over a decade of research working with many people and groups using ImageSnippets to construct graph data about their personal and/or domain-specific image collections. These graphs have become multi-modal representations of personal knowledge interwoven with the images and their descriptions.

  • images can provide starting points for identifying important concepts in personal/domain specific ontology engineering and
  • images can ground concepts with visual representations of those concepts, and this can be particularly valuable in collaborative knowledge-engineering environments.

Optimizing Image Description

Captioning, Classifying, Labeling, Engineering Knowledge, Machine Learning

Image description is hard. Despite some remarkable success in many areas of machine learning. Many of the datasets used over the past decade to improve deep learning algorithms, have become corrupted, overused, and stripped of original metadata that might have helped with new directions in contemporary machine learning. To be able to combine all of the metadata clues to help interpret images is important in moving AI to the next level with hybrid techniques around the context and reasoning.



Multi-Modal and Scene Graphs Augmented by and for Machine Learning. Our techniques combine the conceptual interpretation of text descriptions with image content.

Transforming Classifiers into Knowledge Graphs --- Machine Learning often classifies images with lists of superfluous and unrelated classifiers - often with similar confidence on conflicting concepts. Changing classifiers into graph data yields greater precision, inference capabilities. Active learning...discernment....etc

Accessibility ---- We provide solutions for annotating images with WCAG compliant alt-text. Our system ensures that descriptive data travels with images using industry-standard metadata. All of our systems are managed by, with and for humans who are at the center of our computing solutions.

AI Blindspot Alignment --- Humans and machines both make mistakes - BUT, they also both have areas they excel at. Sometimes the machines spot things that humans miss and humans understand things that machines haven't learned yet. To fix these issues, our system brings both ML detectors into real-world data environments so blindspots can be discovered, graphed and aligned, while also allowing humans to fill their own blindspots with machine interpretations.
